ChurroScope

Churroscope is a combination of words derived from three distinctive terms, Churro, Scope, and Cope. Churro is a fried dough snack originating from Spain and was created by Spanish Shepherds, in which many believed that the name "Churro" was derived from the breed of sheep called Navajo-Churro, as the horns of it resembled the fried dough churro.
